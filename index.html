Milestones

1968

Intel Founded

Gordon Moore and Robert Noyce start Intel.

Intel begins as a memory company, laying the foundation for modern computing.

1971

First Microprocessor

The Intel 4004 changes computing forever.

The world’s first commercial microprocessor puts computing power on a single chip.

1993

Pentium Era

Intel becomes a household name.

The Pentium brand establishes Intel as the dominant processor company.
